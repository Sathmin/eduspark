Privacy Policy

At EduSpark, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information:

Information Collection

We only collect necessary information to provide our services, such as basic account details and usage statistics.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely to enhance your learning experience.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

Cookies

Our Vision

EduSpark was founded with a clear vision: to democratize access to quality education for all students preparing for their examinations.

We believe that every student deserves equal opportunities to succeed, regardless of their location or economic background. Our platform brings together the best educational resources, expert guidance, and innovative learning tools in one accessible place.

Our mission is to transform the traditional learning experience by leveraging technology to create an engaging, personalized, and effective educational environment.

Quality Education

Providing high-quality, accessible education to all students.

Innovation

Accessibility
